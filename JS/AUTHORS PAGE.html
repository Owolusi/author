Owolusi Lucky

BIO

BIO: Owolusi Lucky writes from Nigeria, he was top finalist for 2021
of Peace intercontinental poetry competition, he has published in
Noctivagant press, Crosscurrent, America Diversity Report, Decolonial
Passage, Afrorep, Arkorewrite, Hallowzine, Scars Publication, others are
forthcoming at Macromicrocosm, Dietmilkmag, Sweetycat press anthology,
Collegeville institute, and Solarpunk anthology . He enjoys Natural
history and philosophy.
